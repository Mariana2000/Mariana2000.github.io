This site uses [Google Analytics 4](https://analytics.google.com/) to understand general traffic patterns, such as page views and referral sources. Google may use cookies or similar technologies. You can read Google's privacy documentation for more details.

### Visitor counters and maps

The site footer may load third-party scripts for visit counting (Busuanzi) and visitor map display (ClustrMaps). These services may collect basic usage data such as IP address, browser type, and pages viewed.

### Comments

This site does not currently use a comment system.

### Log files

Like most websites, the hosting provider may record standard server logs, including IP address, browser type, referring page, and access time. This information is used for site operation and security, not to identify individual visitors.
